How to set your photos public

Currently this export tool can only operate on your public Flickr uploads. Here is how you can batch update your private photos and videos:

  1. Go to the Flickr Organize page
  2. Click "more options" on the bottom bar
  3. Click the dropdown "No privacy/SafeSearch filter"
  4. Choose "only show content visible to friends"
  5. If any items load, click "select all"
  6. Click and drag the items up to batch edit them
  7. Click the "permissions" dropdown
  8. Choose "Who can see, comment, tag?"
  9. Select on "Anyone (public)"
  10. Click "Change permissions"
  11. Once the batch update finishes click "Thanks!"
  12. Go back to step 4 and repeat for "family," "friends & family," and "private" settings
